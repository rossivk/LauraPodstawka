MY STORY

Hi, I am Laura.

Very shortly after starting CrossFit, I found that my true passion was weightlifting. It wasn’t long before I started pursuing weightlifting exclusively and for the past two and a half years, I’ve committed all my training to compete in weightlifting.

The progression to coaching has been natural especially after over a decade as an educator. I began coaching weightlifting classes in a CrossFit box and coaching and programming individual athletes, which led me to achieve my British Weightlifting Coaching Level 2.

‘It’s not really about lifting weights or even sport – it’s about relationships, about the inexpressible joy of success and the fulfilment of hard work and being a positive influence on the people around you, not matter how trivial the avenue may seem at times.’ Greg Everett
