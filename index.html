MY STORY

Hi, I am Laura.

I was first introduced to Olympic Weightlifting while taking part in Crossfit. It didn’t take me long to discover that the lifting part of my training was something I really enjoyed.

At the end of 2019, I decided to take part in a few small local weightlifting competitions to see if it was something I wanted to peruse, and at the beginning of 2020 I decided to solely focus on training and competing in weightlifting to see how I could go within the sport.

Following that decision, I had several successful competitions including:

  • 2022 1st Colchester Weightlifting Spring Open
  • 2022 1st British Championship Masters W35 87kg
  • 2021 3rd British Open Senior 87kg
  • 2021 3rd Chelmsford Weightlifting Autumn Open
  • 2021 1st Canadian Masters Championship W35 87kg+
  • 2021 1st British Virtual Championship Masters W35 87kg+
  • 2021 1st B.G.W.L.C Southern Masters
  • 2020 2nd LOWA Open Series
  • 2019 2nd LOWA Open Series
